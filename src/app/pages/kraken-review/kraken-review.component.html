Why Choose Kraken?

Extensive Trading Options (Kraken — UK)

Kraken offers a broad set of market products for UK users: core spot markets, margin-enabled spot positions for eligible accounts, and derivatives via Kraken Perps / Kraken Futures. Availability and per-product limits depend on your verification level and regional eligibility — always confirm live details on Kraken’s product pages.

Spot trading

Kraken’s spot markets let you buy, sell and custody cryptocurrencies directly via orderbooks. The exchange lists hundreds of supported coins and tokens across spot and Pro markets; asset availability can vary by country and by network.

Margin (spot margin)

Margin trading on Kraken allows eligible users to borrow funds to increase position size on spot pairs. Leverage levels vary by pair, but Kraken’s margin product documentation shows commonly available leverage up to around 5–10× depending on the market and pair — exact max leverage is shown per pair in Kraken’s margin tables. UK clients must meet specific eligibility rules (for example declaring high net worth borrower status) to use margin with fiat and fiat-backed stablecoins. Always verify eligibility and per-pair limits in Kraken Pro.

Perpetuals & Futures (Derivatives)

Kraken provides perpetual futures (“Perps”) and broader futures products via Kraken Perps / Kraken Futures. Perps on Kraken advertise up to 10× buying power for retail/Perps products, designed to let traders take directional positions without an expiry; Kraken’s derivatives platform documentation also notes that certain professional/institutional contracts can offer higher leverage (support docs reference up to 50× for specific derivative offerings). Access to derivatives is eligibility-gated and may be restricted by jurisdiction. Derivatives carry high risk — margin, funding rates and liquidation mechanics apply.

How Does Kraken Work?

Kraken is more than just a cryptocurrency exchange — it's a platform designed to help UK traders navigate the crypto market safely and efficiently. From creating your account to executing your first trade, here's how Kraken works for UK users.

How to Sign Up on Kraken UK

Getting started on Kraken is surprisingly straightforward, even if you're new to crypto. UK users begin by visiting the Kraken registration page, where you'll enter a valid email, choose a username, and create a strong password. After agreeing to the terms of service and privacy policy, a confirmation link will be sent to your inbox.

Clicking this link gives you access to a basic Kraken account, which lets you explore the platform. However, to trade crypto in the UK or deposit GBP, you'll need to verify your identity — a necessary step to comply with financial regulations and keep your account secure.

At this stage, it's worth noting: Kraken's user interface is designed to guide you smoothly through registration, making it accessible for both beginners and more experienced traders.
