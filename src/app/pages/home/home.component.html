Our Review Methodology

We assess cryptocurrency exchanges through hands-on, real-world testing. Each review covers 50+ checks grouped into five key pillars to deliver accurate and impartial scores.

How We Test

Each exchange undergoes a 30-day evaluation in which our experts perform real trades, test funding and withdrawals, contact customer support, review security controls, and assess overall usability. We refresh reviews quarterly to reflect product changes and performance.
